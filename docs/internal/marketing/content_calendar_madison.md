## Brand Voice Reminders

- Knowledgeable guide, not salesperson
- Technical depth + accessible explanation
- Real examples over abstract theory
- Direct, no filler phrases
- ASCII-only in all files (no smart quotes, em dashes, or Unicode symbols)
- Never use: "revolutionize", "synergies", "leverage", "next-gen", "game-changing"
- Use: concrete numbers, before/after comparisons, honest limitations
